# Color system

DeepUI uses Radix-style 12-step color scales, exposed through the library's token
variables.

## Understanding the scale

The chart below follows the same high-level composition model used by Radix
Colors:

- Steps `1-2` are background layers.
- Steps `3-5` are component surfaces.
- Steps `6-8` are borders and focus lines.
- Steps `9-10` are solid fills.
- Steps `11-12` are text colors.
